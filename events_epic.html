Bid or Bad

Description

If you are good at generating ideas to real world problems, if you can pitch your ideas and convince people, if you know how to invest your money wisely; then this is the place for you to show your entrepreneurial skills and win the bid.

How the event goes:

1. Every team will be provided with an initial amount which they can use to invest in their own idea as well as in other’s ideas.

2. Each team will be given a problem statement, team members have to discuss the solution to the problem and pitch the idea in front of judge panel and other teams.

3. The pitching team has to come up with an amount to invest in their own idea.

4. Pitching will be followed by an auction round in which other teams have a chance to invest some amount of money in the idea of pitching team.

5. The team with highest bid gets a partnership in the idea.

6. After all the teams have finished pitching and bidding, another round of bidding will be held for all the teams. The team with the cumulative highest wins the bid. (priority will be given to the team who won the first round of bidding for the given team in case of tie).

7. A list will be prepared on the basis of investment received and the pitching for each team.

8. The list will decide the percentage of increment/decrement in the amount invested in the ideas. The percentage will be beared by the teams who invested in the idea. Amount will be returned to the teams with the respective percentage of increment/decrement.

9. The team with the highest amount after the return of investment wins.

Team size

2-5 members

Prizes

1st Position : 1200

2nd Position : 800

Timings

Sunday, 4th Nov 2018; 3:00 pm to 6:00 pm

Sell me this Pen

Description

Marketing is not an easy job. It involves promotion of the product, studying the market, creating a need for the product, keep your product ahead in the competition and advertising it in various ways possible to make sure that the customer notices it in the market. So, take the challenge, step into the shoes of an advertising company, come up with innovative methods to market your product using various strategies, skills and creativity to attract the customers.

Team size

3-8 members

Prizes

1st Position : 2250

2nd Position : 1750

How the event goes:

Round-1(day-1)

  • Teams participate as marketing companies. They will be given a name and also a product to market. The team name will be the name of their company.

Round-2(day 1-2-3 between round 1 and round 3)

  • Teams have to come up with a logo of their company, which will be posted by EPIC club on their instagram handle. Pen points will be awarded based on the no. of likes earned on each logo.
  • Each team will be given a slot in corridor (near H03) where they have a chance to decorate and show their creativity and advertise their product with their company name. This decorations in corridor will also earn ‘pen points’ for each company.
  • Each team has to advertise its product with different and creative techniques. These techniques can involve publicizing your product by showing its different uses to people and judges, preparing slogans, singing jingles, making posters/pamphlets, skits, street plays and other creative ideas. Different ideas, strategies, creative uses to market the product will earn more pen points.

Round-3(day-3)

  • Each team will be given a stage time of 20 mins where teams have to present their company history, company projects, challenges faced, current projects, strategies for marketing etc. which can be totally imaginary and in any kind of form eg: through video, ppt, song, skit etc.
  • The stage time can also be used to show the different uses of product, skit, slogans, jingles, advertisements etc. to the panel of judges and other audience. Note that more types of marketing skills attracts judges and thus leads to earn more pen points.
  • The teams having the highest no. of cumulative pen points will be declared the winner.

Team Size

3-8 members

Prizes

  • 1st Position: Rs.2250
  • 2nd Position: Rs.1750

Timings

  • Round 1: Friday, 2nd Nov 2018; 8:00 pm to 9:00 pm.
  • Round 2: Informals, will be done based on the team’s convenience between the first and third round.
  • Round 3: Sunday, 4th Nov 2018; 7:00 pm to 10:00 pm.
