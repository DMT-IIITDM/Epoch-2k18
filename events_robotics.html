Wire Loop Game

Description

A wire loop game is a game which involves guiding a metal loop (a 'probe') along a serpentine length of wire without touching the loop to the wire. The loop and wire are connected to a power source in such a way that, if they touch, they form a closed electric circuit. The circuit is connected to a light or sound-emitting device of some sort, so that when the loop and the wire touch, the light-emitting device will light up, and the sound-emitting device will make a sound, traditionally a buzzing noise.

Pied Piper

TASK

Level 1: A line follower bot has to be built such a way it traverse the path quickest
Level 2: The bot has to find and traverse in the correct path.

BOT STRUCTURE

  1. Bot dimensions - 30 x 30 x 30 cm
  2. Robots must not use ready-made mechanisms, lego kits, etc. However, readymade sensors and microcontroller kits can be used.
  3. Voltage level at any point should not exceed 24V.

ARENA SPECIFICATION

  1. The line to be traced is of width 4 cm.
  2. Line is Black and surrounding is white.

Level 1

Level 2

POINTS ALLOCATION

  • 40% Weightage for Level 1 and 60% for Level 2
  • Level 1 Marks Distribution:

    1000+(100-t)*5, Where ‘t’ is time required to complete the track in seconds. Max time allowed is 100 secs. After that you will lose 5 points per extra second. If you finish it before 100 seconds, 5 points per second will be awarded as Bonus.

  • Level 2 Marks Distribution:

    Point A (100) + Point B (200) + Point C (300) + Point D (400)+ Endpoint (500)+(600-t), where ‘t’ is time required to complete the track in seconds. Max time allowed is 10 min.

Extra Info

If the bot topples or loses control in the middle of track, a reset at the previous track point is done with a loss of points(manually).

First Reset -40
Second Reset -80
Third Reset -160
And so forth ...

Rules and Regulations

  • A team can consist of a maximum of five members.
  • The line to be traced (black line) is of 4 cm in width.
  • Dimensions should be restricted to the measurements mentioned above; there will be a dimension check before the start of the event.
  • Any damage caused to the arena at any point of time will lead to disqualification/penalty.
  • If the robot is manually touched except at the start, then it will be considered as reset.
  • Teams are not allowed to replace parts between runs. They are, however, allowed to replace defective sensors, but with a penalty added to it.
  • The participants must take note that time will not be paused while resetting the bot.
  • It is forbidden to use a higher voltage than 24 V in the robot.
  • Teams using material other than the given material would be disqualified.
  • No disputes would be entertained on any issue. The decision of the Club Cores is final and binding in all circumstances.
  • The organizers reserve the rights to modify the above rules. Any change in rules will be informed beforehand.

Tug of War/Robo Sumo

TASK

The robot should be capable of performing both the following tasks.

Task A

The two bots in the arena are suppose to pull a knot towards themself, made at the center of the rope, which is tied to the frame of the chase of the bot. A line will be present on the floor as centerline and two other finish line(one for each player) 20 cm apart on either side from center line

Task B

Two Robots in arena attempting to push the other out of a circle. The Bot who pushes the other out of circle will be the winner.

Bot Structure and Specs

  • Dimension : 30x30x30 cm
  • Weight : 1Kg max
  • The bot should not use a ready made mechanism, Lego kit, etc.(it should be your creativity)
  • The weight of bot must be 1 kg.
  • If it is more then required dimension will not be able to perform.
  • The source voltage should not exceed 24V.


    • Arena specifications

    Tug of War

    • Distance between finish line and Centre line will be 20 cm.
    • Floor will be tiled.

    Robo Sumo

    • Radius of the arena is 1m.
    • Floor will be tiled.

    Rules and Regulations

    Tug of War

    • The knot will be present in the middle of the rope .
    • At the start of the war, the knot will be exactly above the center line marked on the arena.
    • In the middle of the match if the hook in which rope is tied gets loose/break the the bot will lose points/match.
    • Both bots should start pulling the rope simultaneously, the team who manage to pull the knot to finish line will win.
    • The bot going out of the given dimensions are not allowed to take part in the competition.
    • Max 3 participants are allowed.
    • There should be a hook on chase of robot to tie the rope.

    Robo Sumo

    • The robot should have a plane surface in front ,from where it can perform pushing .
    • In the middle of match if any damage occurs the bot will lose points.
    • Both Robot should start the war simultaneous.
    • Max 3 participants are allowed.
