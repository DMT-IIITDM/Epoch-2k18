Introduction to Automobile Engineering Simulations - Workshop

Description

This workshop is aimed to guide participants how to move from designing to analysis phase. Industries require proper analysis and simulations before actual fabrication. And the same goes for BAJA participation. So here, with this, we will be demonstrating our next steps for SAE BAJA preparation. Not only for BAJA, those who only want to learn simulation can also attend. This will be helpful for those who want to test their design before actual fabrication.

Software

Ansys

Requirements

Laptop installed with Ansys. Designs to be simulated will be provided on-site. (For those without laptop, a lab with Ansys installed will be arranged.)

Completely free of cost! Only genuine interest is required to join our team.
